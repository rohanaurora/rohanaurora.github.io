British Longhair Cat

The British Longhair is a mixed breed cat – a cross between the British Shorthair and Persian cat breeds. Smoothie, the cat, dubbed “The Most Photogenic Cat In The World”, is Instagram's feline goddesses.

  • Scientific Name — Felis catus
  • Average Length — 12-14 inches
  • Average Lifespan — 15-17 years
  • Average Weight — 9-18 lbs

The British Longhair breeds are suited for any cat loving family, individuals who work a lot, families with kids and other pets. Their temperament is friendly, affectionate, independent, laid-back.
